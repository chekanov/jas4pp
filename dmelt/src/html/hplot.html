HPlot help

Location of points:
to show the mouse location in the USER or NDC coordinates, use the middle mouse button.

Zoom in::
Method (a): Locate the axis with the mouse, press the middle button and drug the mouse. A red line should indicate the zoom region.

Method (b): Double-click with middle mouse button and drug the pointer.

Changing margins::
Navigate the mouse to the left, right, top, bottom margin and press the right mouse button.
